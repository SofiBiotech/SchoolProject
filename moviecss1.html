Disney's Hercules (1997)

Hercules is a 1997 animated musical fantasy film produced by Walt Disney Feature Animation. The story is based on Greek mythology and follows the journey of Hercules, the son of Zeus, as he tries to prove himself worthy of living among the gods on Mount Olympus. With the help of his friends and mentor, he faces trials set by the villainous Hades, the god of the Underworld.

Main Cast

  • Tate Donovan as Hercules
  • Danny DeVito as Philoctetes (Phil
  • James Woods as Hades
  • Susan Egan as Meg
  • Rip Torn as Zeus
